Canada lynx

A specialist predator, the Canada lynx depends heavily on the snowshoe hare for food.

  • Scientific Name: Felis lynx canadensis
  • Average Length: 2.5 - 3.5 ft. (adult)
  • Average Lifespan: 10-14 years
  • Habitat: Alaska, Canada, northern United States

The Canada lynx is characterized by its long, dense fur, triangular ears with black tufts at the tips, and broad, snowshoe-like paws.
